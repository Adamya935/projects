Ayurvedic Remedies for Stomach Ache

Ginger and Honey

Mix half a teaspoon of ginger juice with honey to help relieve bloating and gas.

Cumin Seeds and Rock Salt

Roast cumin seeds and mix with warm water and rock salt to soothe the stomach and balance pH levels.

Mint Solution

Replace carbonated drinks with mint extracts to reduce stomach irritation.

Turmeric and Ginger Tea

Brew raw ginger and turmeric in water, reducing to half. Drink once daily to help heal stomach inflammation.
