PEPPERMINT
Peppermint is another natural remedy known to alleviate nausea. Its menthol content helps relax the muscles of the gastrointestinal tract, which can reduce symptoms of nausea and vomiting. Here are a few ways you can use peppermint for nausea relief.
Peppermint tea: Steep a peppermint tea bag or a teaspoon of dried peppermint leaves in hot water for about 10 minutes. Sip the tea slowly.

LEMON
Lemons contain citric acid, a naturally-occurring compound thought to aid digestion and soothe the stomach. Try adding freshly-squeezed lemon juice to water and sip throughout the day.
Cut a lemon into two halves. Sprinkle a very small pinch of black salt over the cut surface, and lick it. The strong aroma of lemon coupled with the salty taste will make you feel better.
